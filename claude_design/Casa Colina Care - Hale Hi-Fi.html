Our story

This started with my grandmother.

I watched her spend her last years somewhere that felt like a waiting room. Fluorescent lights. Shift changes. A TV nobody watched. I couldn't change it for her — but I could build something different for the next family.

Casa Colina is six bedrooms, one kitchen, and a lanai that catches the trade winds. It's small because care can't be scaled.

We cook real meals. We sit on the lanai. We call families by their first names and we mean it. If that sounds like what your mother, father, or spouse deserves — come visit. We'll put water on for tea.
