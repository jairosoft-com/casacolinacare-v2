§ 02 — Verifiable through state DOH

Casa Colina operates as a state-licensed Adult Residential Care Home (ARCH Type II) under §11-100.1 of the Hawai‘i Administrative Rules. We submit to annual licensure renewal and unannounced DOH inspections, maintain professional liability coverage, and require certifications for every caregiver.
